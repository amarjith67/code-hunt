9.You have N pills of characteristic values a1,a2….an. On each day i, you can take the i th pill and increase your health by ai points H:=H+ai or Do nothing and take 1 point of damage H:=H−1 , where H is a value denoting your health. You die if your health becomes zero. Initially H=10 . What is the minimum number of pills you should take to survive for N days.

Sample Input :

5
2 3 1 5 4

Sample Output :

3
